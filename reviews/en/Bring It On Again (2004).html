I actually managed to find the first Bring It On movie somewhat entertaining. It had good actors, well some... Well, one... Anyway, it had a decent script and an overall sense of fun to it. Like the director went: "We all know this is not exactly a Godfather in the making, so let's just have fun with it." And it worked.

This movie however, is an entirely different story. If it was possible for vomit to pass through ear-canals I am certain there would have been residue on my shoulders after watching this atrocity. This is not a movie. This is a torture device constructed by ex-nazi scientists in a desperate attempt to leviate some of their sexual frustration in these metrosexual times.

The only reason this movie gets one star... Is something I am trying to remember while writing this... Actually I don't recall anything of interest in this piece of shit. No wait! There's a locker-room scene.
