About JGE Mainframe

I created this site because I have a dream. A dream that the days of the SCI-FI MMO are far from over. A dream that JGE might be what we've all been waiting for. Oh yes, I have a dream. Ever since the golden years of Anarchy Online were over, the thirst, nay, the hunger for something new has kept us all searching. Here's hoping it's finally here, in Jumpgate Evolution.

Apart from serving as a hub for news and rumours, this site will contain (what yours truly considers) essential information. This information will be presented in a manner first and foremost suited for the experienced MMO gamer. Thus the tagline "Jumpgate Evolution Pro Resource". I don't have anything against nubs, but I aim to cater to people who've been playing MMO's for a while. Mainly because that means I can write less and link up theorycraft without explaining what it is. Do you feel me?

At the time of writing this the game hasn't even entered closed beta yet. Obviously the content of the site will be sparse until I have gotten my hands on a copy and, gasp, played for a few hours. I do hope, however, that you enjoy what little content there is. Send me tips on Twitter, thanks.

– Alexander
