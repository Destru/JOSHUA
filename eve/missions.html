Mission Runner Skills

Social Skill at social interaction, 5% bonus to NPC standing increase.
Connections Skill at interacting with friendly NPC's, 4% bonus to effective standing.
Negotiation Skill at agent negotiation, 5% additional pay for agent missions.
When the missions are starting to get too easy; Train Social to level 3, then get Connections 1/2. You'll notice a solid boost to your standings. I could start doing good quality level 2-missions almost immideately afterwards.
